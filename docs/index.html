Hello! I'm Paul.

Welcome to my corner of the web.

I'm an aspiring writer. About a year after I graduated from college, I read On the Road, and I was so inspired I decided to try and make a career of it. I've had a heck of a time making it work, but that's how it goes in this racket.

During the day, I work as a frontend software engineer. That means I spend my days coding web interfaces. Currently I'm working at a company called Iterable. Before that I was at Accenture. And before that, I actually worked as a biologist, doing bird surveys in different parts of the U.S. I worked for outfits like USGS, Point Blue, and Bird Conservancy of the Rockies. I got to see a lot of the country, and learned a few things about the land and the people who live on it.

When I'm not working, I love to go hiking and birdwatching, learn jiu jitsu, and spend time with my friends and family.
